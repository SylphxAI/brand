## Full About — EN

### Who we are

**Sylphx Limited** builds **Sylphx** — an AI-native application platform — and contributes open infrastructure under **SylphxAI**. The product promise is consolidation without dumbing down: auth, data, AI, jobs, deploy, and more behind **one SDK**, with **dedicated Postgres and managed cache per project**, not soft multi-tenant theatre.

### Why we exist

The modern starter stack is a tax. Before the first customer, teams pay in money, context switches, and integration debt — Vercel here, Supabase there, Clerk over there, model APIs somewhere else. Agent systems make the tax worse: every tool has a different identity, isolation, and bill.

Sylphx exists so builders **stop stitching tools together** and ship on a coherent substrate.

### What we believe

1. **One platform beats twelve partial truths.**  
2. **Isolation should be real.** Per-project data boundaries you can reason about.  
3. **Idle should be cheap.** Hibernate when nothing runs; wake fast when it does.  
4. **AI is infrastructure.** A gateway is a service, not a blog post.  
5. **Open proof matters.** Libraries and MCP tools show how we engineer.

### Customer Zero

We run our own agent and product systems on Sylphx. The platform is not theoretical.

### Who we’re for

Indie builders, startups, and AI product engineers who want speed without a frankenstack; teams that care about isolation and cost when projects sleep; developers who live in MCP and TypeScript ecosystems.

### Who we’re not for

Buyers seeking a single-purpose chatbot skin, or enterprises that need a multi-year ERP replacement story on day one. We are a platform for builders who ship software.
